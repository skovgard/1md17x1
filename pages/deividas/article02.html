Two websites: a visual designer's armoury

By David C

Color is vital for visual design. However, there are so many of them that it is hard to keep track. Not anymore though!

ColorHexa is presented as the encyclopedia of color. It provides much needed information and conversion

The website covers many things regarding each color. For instance - hex codes, RGB values CMYK and more. Moreover, you are provided with color schemes with your colors for complementary, analogous any other color.

ColorHexa also shows how your color of your choice is perceived by people affected by color deficiency

Don't miss out!

Moving on to labs.500px.com - online photography community's marketplace.

What is interesting about this website is that instead of using keywords to search one has to use color!

It is simple and fun to use. First you pick color and size of brush. Then Splash paint on the canvas and see matching images appear. Simply amazing!

You get access to search and find high quality photos according to your needs. Then you can buy and download the photos you like.
