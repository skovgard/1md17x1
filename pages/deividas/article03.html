Three websites for anyone's liking. Really!

By David C

First there is findtheinvisiblecow.com - name says it all!

Very simple to use. Just move your cursor around to find the cow. The shouting indicates how close it is. Just like HotnCold game from childhood but online! Make sure your audio is on!

Second, we have the Sideways Dictionary. It is like a dictionary... But instead of definitions you get analogies

Let's take the term "2 Factor Authentication" . One the first analogy says:

"It’s like Cinderella’s slipper. She can give her name and confirm where she was before midnight, but it’s only when the slipper fits that Prince Charming knows she’s for real. The Prince was an early adopter of 2FA."

Pretty neat website, eh?

The third and last one is Terms of Service; Didn't Read. No one reads the terms of service. This website makes a short version of them.

https://tosdr.org/
